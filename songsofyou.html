"Heat Waves" - Glass Animals
"Sometimes all I think about is you." Whenever this song plays, I think about you without fail. Specifically, I think about that time when we had first started going on dates. Even though that was only like 2 years ago, it still kind of makes me feel nostalgic. The first semester after I had graduated, I had a really hard time focusing in class because you were on mind all the time.

"Something About You" - Eyedress
"She looks just like a dream, the prettiest girl I've ever seen." I remember posting this song in the background to some pictures of us on Prom night. You really did look like you belonged on the cover of a magazine. It really couldn't \ have been more fitting. The song isn't anything crazy, and that's how I feel when I'm around you, comfortable and relaxed.

"Lose" - KSI
"She loves me, she loves me not, patience, I'm runnin' out. Walkin' all over me, made me her stompin' grounds." I listened to this song all the time around the time that we had barely started talking. I couldn't really tell if you also liked me or if you were just being friendly. Not gonna lie, this drove me crazy, I had no idea what to think. KSI is a youtuber so he isn't a very talented musician, but this song that he did with Lil Wayne is spot on, it perfectly captures how I felt at this time.

"Del Rio" - La Fe Norteña
"Yo sé que te tengo pero no te tengo cerca de mí. Siento que algo falta y eso que me falta eres tú." So technically the song was originally made by a guy named Ed Maverick, but I prefer this version because the original sounds too depressing. This song reminds me of how you are going to leave soon for school. I always get a little sad when I really listen to this song. I felt it was important to mention this song.

"Here With Me" - D4vd
"I don't care how long it takes, as long as I'm with you, I've got a smile on my face." This is a really relaxing song too, it makes me think about our future together. If I'm being honest, I feel like I've been getting really old, and it's kind of scary, like damn, I'm already in my 20's? It still feels like I was in high school just a few months ago. Despite all this, the thought of growing old together makes me feel better about getting older.
